# About MediSpace E-Commerce Backend

MediSpace E-Commerce Backend is the API, realtime, and integration layer for a digital pharmacy platform. It powers product catalog management, checkout, prescriptions, pharmacist consultation, AI chat, search, recommendations, loyalty, coupons, community features, moderation, payments, shipping, and LiveKit video health events.

The service is built with Node.js, Express, TypeScript, MongoDB, Redis, Typesense, Socket.IO, and Docker. It also coordinates Python microservices for OCR, machine-learning recommendations, and Gemma-based AI chat.
